Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How long does a typical software development project take?
Project timelines vary based on complexity and requirements. Simple applications typically take 2-4 months, while comprehensive ERP systems like Agri-ERP can take 6-12 months. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.
Do you provide training and support after project completion?
Yes! We provide comprehensive training for all users and 24/7 technical support. Our support packages include regular system updates, bug fixes, and feature enhancements based on your evolving needs.
Can you integrate with our existing farm management systems?
Absolutely. We specialize in system integration and can connect our solutions with most existing farm management software, IoT devices, and agricultural equipment. We conduct a thorough analysis of your current systems before proposing integration strategies.
What makes your solutions suitable for middle-aged farmers?
We design our interfaces with simplicity and clarity in mind. Large, clear buttons, intuitive navigation, and comprehensive user guides ensure that farmers of all ages can effectively use our technology. We also provide extensive training and support to ensure smooth adoption.
